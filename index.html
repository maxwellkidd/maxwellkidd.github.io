About Me

I grew up in Northwest Indiana where I attended Boone Grove from kindergarten until I graduated high school in 2018. While at Boone Grove I was the captain and goalkeeper for the varsity soccer program, and completed a Computer Technology Vocational program through the Porter County Career Center.
